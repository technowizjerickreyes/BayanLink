# Visual Enhancements - BayanLink Aliaga Portal

## Strategic Image Integration Summary

Your BayanLink portal has been enhanced with authentic Aliaga municipality images strategically placed to create maximum visual impact and user engagement.

## Images Integrated

### 1. Official Municipal Seal 🛡️
**What it is**: The official emblem of Aliaga featuring mountains, agricultural imagery, and founded date (1849)

**Where it appears**:
- ✨ **Navbar** (Top-left) - Primary branding element visible on every page
- 📄 **About Page** - Municipal profile section (left side, 256px)
- 🔗 **Footer** - Brand reinforcement (96px, desktop only)

**Why it matters**: 
- Immediate visual identification
- Builds official government credibility
- Creates consistent branding throughout the portal

**Impact**: Every visitor instantly knows they're on Aliaga's official municipal portal

### 2. Taong Putik Festival 🎉
**What it is**: Stunning aerial photo of Aliaga's famous mud festival celebration (Saint John the Baptist feast, June 24)

**Where it appears**:
- 🎭 **About Page** - "Culture & Traditions" section (left side, full-width)

**Context provided**:
- Explains unique mud costume tradition
- Describes house-to-house visits by devotees
- Lists festival highlights with teal accent styling
- Religious and cultural significance

**Why it matters**: 
- Showcases unique cultural identity
- Memorable visual storytelling
- Differentiates Aliaga from other municipalities
- Attracts cultural tourism interest

**Impact**: Users remember Aliaga's distinctive cultural heritage

## Visual Design Integration

### Color Coordination
- All images use matching teal (bayan-teal) accent borders
- Consistent rounded corners (rounded-lg) on all images
- Shadow effects (shadow-card) for depth

### Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop**: Full-size images in grid layouts
- **Tablet**: Scaled proportionally, 2-column where applicable
- **Mobile**: Stacked vertically, full-width, readable text spacing

### Accessibility
- Descriptive alt text on all images
- Proper semantic HTML structure
- WCAG compliant styling
